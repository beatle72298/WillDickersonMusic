About

Will Dickerson is a New Orleanian singer/songwriter & guitarist. The youngest in a family of musicians, Will grew up backstage in New Orleans’s vibrant music scene and culture. He uses this unique experience and understanding of the theoretical and culture sides of music to write songs that are refreshingly creative and enthusiastic.

In spite of the pandemic, Will’s uncanny ability to accompany himself on multiple instruments has allowed him to remain one of the few fully operational musical acts in New Orleans, playing weekly at local restaurants/bars and hosting his own porch concert series for his neighborhood.

Often described as falling somewhere between Jimi Hendrix, Bob Dylan and Jeff Buckley, musically, Will Dickerson is not an artist you want miss. “Music is the voice of human emotion and with my music I just want to lend a voice for anyone who feels like words are never enough.”

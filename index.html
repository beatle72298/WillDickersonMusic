About



Jank Setup is a group of five millennials learning music as fast as possible in order to catapult themselves into financial success. The sound of Jank Setup can be likened to a hearty beef stew of funk and RnB, seasoned with soul, and garnished with jazz. At their live shows, the gentlemen of Jank hope to display a high degree of musical craftsmanship, as well as oodles of contagious joy. Come hungry for a heaping plate of colorful brass harmonies, tight-knit rhythmic grooves, and versatile vocal styles.
